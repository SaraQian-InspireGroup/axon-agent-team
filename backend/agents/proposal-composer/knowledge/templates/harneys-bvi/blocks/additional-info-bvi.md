# Additional Information — BVI

## Financial Annual Return (FAR)

The Financial Annual Return must be filed within nine months of the financial year end. FAR preparation may require separate accounting and bookkeeping services depending on the entity's records.

## Economic Substance

Entities carrying on relevant activities must meet economic substance requirements and file annual reports. Fees vary by activity type and complexity.

## Transfer-in

For entities transferring from another agent or jurisdiction, additional registry fees and professional fees may apply. A forced transfer may incur separate registry and professional charges as quoted.

## Physical corporate kit

Dispatch of a physical corporate kit and courier services are optional and quoted separately where required.
